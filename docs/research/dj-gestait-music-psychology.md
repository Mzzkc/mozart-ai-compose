# DJ GestAIt — Music Psychology Research Summary

Research conducted 2026-04-21 to inform the design of DJ GestAIt's performance system.

## Key Findings (Actionable)

### 1. Anticipation IS the Reward (Salimpoor 2011)
Dopamine releases in two separate brain regions: caudate (anticipation) and nucleus accumbens (experience). Buildups are not delays before the payoff — they ARE a separate reward. Budget as much design attention to anticipation as to climactic moments.

### 2. Syncopation Sweet Spot (Witek 2014)
Medium syncopation = maximum groove and pleasure. Low = boring. High = lost beat. The inverted-U is measurable. Target the middle for sustained dance energy. Push higher for tension, pull back for groove.

### 3. Berlyne Curve (1971)
Aesthetic preference follows an inverted-U with complexity. Too simple bores, too complex confuses. Track cumulative complexity across the set. After peaks, retreat to reset the curve. Familiarity shifts the curve — repeated motifs become accessible, enabling higher complexity later.

### 4. Mere Exposure + Variation
Introduce motifs early, bring them back with variations. Creates familiarity within the set itself. But also introduce novelty to prevent satiation. Track "motif exposure count."

### 5. Frisson Triggers (Frontiers 2014)
Specific musical gestures trigger chills: unexpected silences, key changes, sudden textural shifts, lingering pauses before resolution. These are categorizable and deployable. Don't overuse — frequency kills impact.

### 6. EDM Buildup-Breakdown-Drop Cycle
The BBD cycle is the core emotional architecture. Breakdown (stripping elements) is where anticipatory dopamine is HIGHEST. Empty space does neurochemical work. Don't fill it.

### 7. AI Performer Bias (Ansani 2025)
Listeners rate identical music lower when told it's AI. 79% confabulate differences. Cannot be overcome by quality alone. Path: do things humans CANNOT do. Make AI-ness the feature, not the liability.

### 8. Combinatorial Creativity (Doshi & Hauser 2024)
AI's genuine creative strength: novel combinations of known elements. But AI outputs converge (homogeneity risk). Counter: inject variation, different model families, constraint randomization.

### 9. Curation Uncanny Valley
Technical perfection without personality = uncanny valley. Cure: asymmetry, surprise, taste. Deliberate personality, not deliberate imperfection.

### 10. Three Pillars of Liveness
Immediacy (real-time), contingency (could go differently), social co-presence. AI DJ needs all three. Signal liveness by making real-time decisions visible.

### 11. Parasocial Connection
Consistent persona, recognizable preferences, signature moves. Audiences bond with AI performers when character is coherent. Build DJ GestAIt's personality to be recognizable across performances.
